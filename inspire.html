Who is Harry Styles?

Starting his musical career at a young age, Harry Styles was one of the five members of the band One Direction. After having many hit songs in the band, Harry continued to have a very successful career when he became a solo artist.

Over the last few years, Harry Styles has created three albums, the latest coming out last spring; Harry's House. This album has already won many awards and is nominated for many awards in the future. Not only is Harry Styles a successful musician, but he is also an actor and a business owner. Along with his known success, Harry Styles promotes a good message to treat all people with kindness.
